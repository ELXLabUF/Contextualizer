About Contextualizer

Contextualizer is a website developed by a research team at the University of Florida.

Members of the research/development team: Aakash Naik, Yash Goel, Abhishek Kulkarni, Sharon Lynn Chu.

Contextualizer is part of a larger project funded by the National Science Foundation under grant #1942937 Bridging Formal and Everyday Learning through Wearable Technologies: Towards a Connected Learning Paradigm.

For more info, contact Dr. Sharon Lynn Chu at slchu@ufl.edu.
